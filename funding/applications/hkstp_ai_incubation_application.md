## Section 1 — Company & Product Overview

AuraSense Limited is a Hong Kong-based deep-tech startup developing the Spike-based Facade Scanning Vision Codec (SFSVC), a revolutionary neuromorphic AI platform designed specifically for autonomous building facade inspection. The company is tackling the critical public safety challenge of aging urban infrastructure, starting with Hong Kong’s 40,000+ buildings subject to the Mandatory Building Inspection Scheme (MBIS). SFSVC replaces traditional, high-risk rope-access inspection with a sparse, event-driven AI system that processes visual data at the edge with unprecedented efficiency and speed.

Our product is an integrated enterprise platform that combines autonomous drone flight, real-time neuromorphic defect detection, and MBIS-compliant report generation. By leveraging the categorical efficiency of spike-based neural processing, SFSVC delivers a 60% cost reduction and a 10–20× turnaround time improvement compared to incumbent manual methods. AuraSense is currently at TRL 6–7 with a validated prototype and is moving toward full commercial pilot deployment.

## Section 2 — Technology Description

### Neuromorphic Inference & SFSVC
Unlike conventional AI systems built on convolutional neural networks (CNNs) that process every pixel in every frame, SFSVC is architected around neuromorphic spike-based processing. Our system only computes temporal changes (events), dramatically reducing redundant operations. This event-driven approach enables us to run high-accuracy defect detection models on low-power edge hardware without relying on cloud GPU connectivity.

### STDP Adaptive Learning
SFSVC utilizes Spike-Timing-Dependent Plasticity (STDP) for continuous on-site learning. As the drone scans a building, the STDP layer adapts to the specific material textures and lighting conditions of that site in real-time. This ensures that detection accuracy improves autonomously with every building scanned, eliminating the need for constant offline retraining.

### Multi-spectral Fusion & Edge Deployment
Our 8-lane parallel pipeline fuses RGB imagery with thermal signatures and LiDAR point clouds to provide a comprehensive health assessment of the building facade. This multi-modal approach enables the detection of structural cracks, surface spalling, and subsurface delamination. The entire inference workload is optimized for edge deployment, achieving sub-4ms P95 latency and ensuring that sensitive inspection data never leaves the building site.

AuraSense operates a B2B SaaS and licensing model. Our primary customers are Registered Inspector (RI) firms who are the mandatory gatekeepers of the MBIS process.
