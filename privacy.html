Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

AuraSense Limited, registered in Hong Kong SAR.
Contact: support@aurasensehk.com

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to deliver and support your SFSVC license:

  • Identity & contact data — name, company name, work email address (collected via trial signup form or contact requests)
  • Payment data — processed entirely by Stripe. We never receive, store, or have access to card numbers, CVVs, or bank details
  • License usage data — validation timestamps and cryptographic machine fingerprints (SHA-256 hashes) generated by your hardware during activation and periodic check-in
  • Website analytics — page views and referring pages (no personal data, no cross-site tracking)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To deliver and manage your SFSVC license
  • To send license keys, renewal notifications, and essential account emails
  • To respond to support requests
  • To detect and prevent licensing abuse

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

  • Account & contract data — retained while your subscription is active, plus 12 months after cancellation
  • Payment records — managed by Stripe in accordance with their retention policies and applicable financial regulations
  • License validation logs — 90-day rolling window; automatically purged

5. Your Rights (PDPO — Hong Kong)

Under the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486) of Hong Kong SAR, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of personal data where no legitimate business reason requires retention
  • Object to data processing in limited circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, contact support@aurasensehk.com. We will respond within 40 days as required by the Ordinance.

6. Third-Party Processors

We use the following sub-processors, each under contractual data protection obligations:

  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • Keygen.sh — cryptographic license management
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • GitHub — source code and release hosting

7. Cookies

We use essential session cookies only — required for the trial signup flow and website functionality. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or third-party analytics that share data externally.

See our Cookie Policy for details.

8. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. License keys are Ed25519 cryptographically signed. Machine fingerprints are stored as SHA-256 hashes — we cannot reverse-engineer your hardware identity from the hash.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related queries:
support@aurasensehk.com
