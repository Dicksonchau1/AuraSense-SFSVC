Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Trial Cancellation — Zero Charge Guarantee

Cancel before day 7 of your trial: zero charge — guaranteed.
Your license is deactivated at trial end. No refund is needed as no charge was made.

To cancel your trial:

  • Use the Stripe customer portal link in your welcome email, or
  • Email support@aurasensehk.com before your trial end date

We confirm all cancellations by email. If you cancel and are charged in error, we will issue a full refund within 2 business days.

Post-Trial Refund Window

Within 3 calendar days of your first subscription charge (the charge that occurs after the 7-day trial), you may request a full refund if SFSVC does not perform to its documented benchmark specifications on your supported hardware.

Eligibility requirements:

  • Refund request submitted within 3 days of the charge date
  • Hardware specifications provided (CPU model, RAM, OS version)
  • Error output or benchmark result provided showing the issue
  • SFSVC tested on a supported platform: Ubuntu 22.04, x86_64 or ARM64

We process approved refunds within 5 business days. Refunds are returned to the original payment method via Stripe.

Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds are not available:

  • After 3 calendar days from the charge date
  • For trial period usage (no charge was made during trial)
  • For mid-subscription cancellations — your subscription cancels at the end of the current billing period and no further charges are made
  • If SFSVC was used on unsupported hardware or operating systems
  • For change-of-mind after the 3-day window

Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time via the Stripe customer portal. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you will not be charged again, and your license remains active until that date.

Partial-month refunds for mid-period cancellations are not provided unless there is a service failure on our part.

How to Request a Refund

Email support@aurasensehk.com with:

  • Subject: Refund Request — [your registered email]
  • Your Stripe order/invoice ID (from your receipt email)
  • Hardware specifications (if requesting post-trial performance refund)
  • Description of the issue

We will acknowledge your request within 1 business day.
