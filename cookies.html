Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Our Approach

AuraSense uses essential cookies only. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party cookies that share data with external services for marketing or analytics purposes.

What Cookies We Use

We use a single category of cookie:

  • Cookie consent preference — stored in localStorage under the key aurasense-cookie-consent. This records that you have acknowledged our cookie notice so it is not shown on every visit. It contains no personal data — only the value "1".

We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or any behavioural analytics
  • Facebook Pixel or any social media tracking
  • Advertising retargeting cookies
  • Cross-site tracking of any kind
  • Third-party session cookies

Stripe (Payment Processing)

When you proceed to Stripe's hosted checkout, Stripe may set their own cookies on stripe.com to operate the payment form securely. These are set on Stripe's domain, not on www.aurasensehk.com. Stripe's cookie policy applies: stripe.com/cookie-settings

How to Control Cookies

Because we only use essential localStorage (not traditional HTTP cookies), standard browser cookie controls may not affect our storage. To clear our consent record:

  • Open your browser's developer tools
  • Navigate to Application → Local Storage → www.aurasensehk.com
  • Delete the aurasense-cookie-consent key

Or simply clear all site data in your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever introduce additional cookies (for example, to support login functionality), we will update this policy and request fresh consent before setting any new cookies.
