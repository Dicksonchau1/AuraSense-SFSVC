Façade Risk Engine

Built on SFSVC Neuromorphic Core

The Challenge

Façade inspections generate thousands of images and point clouds. Manual review is slow, inconsistent, and difficult to trend across cycles.

The Engine

The façade deployment of SFSVC:

  • Ingests UAV RGB and optional LiDAR data
  • Converts frames into spike-based representation
  • Detects and tracks cracks across tiles and frames
  • Fuses geometric deformation signals
  • Produces structured, MBIS-aligned risk outputs

Note: Processing occurs fully on-site.

Outputs

Colour-coded façade risk maps

Structured defect tables

Severity band classification

Time-series comparison across inspections

Deployment
  • Linux edge node (mini PC or appliance)
  • Offline-first architecture
  • Optional integration with reporting systems

The façade engine is a vertical configuration built on the SFSVC SDK.
