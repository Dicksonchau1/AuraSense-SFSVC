How SFSVC works

A three-stage pipeline that turns raw drone footage into actionable structural intelligence — all at the edge.

1

Delta-Spike Encoding

Frame-to-frame pixel differences are computed on-device. Only regions with meaningful change (cracks, spalls, anomalies) are flagged for transmission — eliminating 94% of redundant data.

2

Adaptive YOLO Detection

A custom YOLOv8-nano model runs inference on flagged regions. Confidence-weighted bounding boxes identify cracks, corrosion, and structural degradation in real-time on CPU.

3

Visual Confirmation Stream

Only annotated key-frames with detection overlays are transmitted to the ground station. Human operators see confirmed findings instantly — not raw video noise.

4

Failsafe & Degraded Mode

If signal drops or hardware degrades, SFSVC automatically enters degraded-mode: lowering resolution, increasing compression, and preserving critical detections until conditions improve.
