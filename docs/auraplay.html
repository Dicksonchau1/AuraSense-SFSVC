How It Works

AI-powered phonics in three steps

AuraPlay combines computer vision, speech processing, and gamification to create an engaging learning experience.

📷

1. Camera Tracks Lips

The app uses your device camera to detect facial landmarks and track lip movements in real-time — all processing happens on-device.

🎧

2. AI Matches Phonemes

Our model compares the child's lip shape and audio to expected phoneme patterns, identifying pronunciation gaps with 70%+ accuracy.

⭐

3. Instant Feedback

Children receive immediate, encouraging visual and audio feedback — under 50ms latency — keeping them engaged and building confidence.

Features

Designed for young learners

Every feature is built with children aged 4-10 in mind — intuitive, safe, and research-informed.

Gamified Learning

Points, streaks, and animated characters turn phonics practice into a game children actually want to play. Built-in rewards keep motivation high.

Browser-Based

No app store downloads. No installation hassles. AuraPlay runs entirely in the browser — open the link and start learning in seconds.

Privacy-First

All camera processing happens on-device. No video is recorded, uploaded, or stored. Children's data never leaves their device.

Adaptive Difficulty

The app adjusts phoneme complexity based on each child's progress. Struggling readers get extra support; confident ones advance faster.
