About AuraSense

AuraSense builds neuromorphic perception infrastructure for edge autonomy. Founded in Hong Kong, we focus on deterministic, CPU-native perception systems designed to operate without cloud or GPU dependency.

Our first vertical deployment addresses structural inspection workflows. The SFSVC SDK is designed for broader robotics and industrial edge applications.

We believe critical perception systems should operate where decisions are made — at the edge.
