Request Technical Brief

Infrastructure, Not Software

Inspection fleets generate continuous video streams. Transmission cost scales linearly. Latency variability creates operational risk.

SFSVC restructures the pipeline at the codec layer. Converts video into sparse, control-relevant signals before cloud ingestion.

Deterministic control preserved. Transmission load reduced. Auditability preserved.

This is infrastructure. Not an overlay.
